Institutions Accreditation

ACTD Institutional accreditation is a system of review of an institution according to ACTD standards. It provides public assurance about the quality of institutions, and it provides a mechanism for the training provider institutions to evaluate themselves and to improve.

Programs Accreditation

Our accreditation services are the process by which the professional training program in various fields is evaluated in accordance with the standards set by ACTD. When we verify that the training program complies with these standards, our accreditation for the program certificates is granted.
