Institutional Accreditation Application

Once you have all the necessary information and are ready to proceed with the CTSDA accreditation process, please fill out the form below. Our team will review your submission and contact you via the email address provided.

Please enter the full name of your institution or company.

Specify the legal status (e.g., Government Department, Private Company, Association, LLC, etc.).

Provide the registration number of your institution or company. We will request a copy of the registration document later.

Indicate the country where your institution or company is registered.

Enter the complete physical address of your institution or company.

Provide the official email address of your institution or company.

Enter the name of the person submitting this application.

Specify the position of the person submitting this application (e.g., Director, Chairman, Owner, Quality Manager, Accreditation Manager).

Include the mobile phone number with the international code. Preferably, this number should be reachable via WhatsApp.

Enter the date when your institution or company was established.

Describe the primary training areas offered by your institution or company.

Detail the types of certificates your institution or company issues.

Explain the conditions for accepting trainees at your institution or company.

Provide an approximate total number of training programs conducted each year.

State the total number of staff members.

Indicate the number of trainees enrolled each year.

Provide the URL of your institution's or company's website. An active website is crucial for accreditation.

Enter the links to your institution's or company's social media accounts. Active social media presence is important for accreditation.

If available, provide details of any local accreditation documents.

If available, provide details of any international accreditation documents.

By submitting this application, you agree to the following:
  • cover all fees and costs associated with the accreditation process.
  • commit to providing unrestricted access to information, documents, and records as required for assessment and maintenance of accreditation.
  • If you have any questions, please contact us before submitting your request. We will respond to all inquiries.
  • Do not submit an accreditation request unless you are certain about obtaining accreditation.
